Welcome to Durga Campus

Shree Durga Campus is a public higher educational institution located in Manara, Mahottari district of Nepal. It is affiliated to Tribhuvan University for bachelor degree programs and approved by University Grants Commission of Nepal. Shree Durga Campus offers bachelor degree programs such as Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS). This campus also provides scholarship facility for marginalized and economically needy students.

Academics (Curriculum Overview)

Durga Secondary School, Mahottari (दुर्गा माध्यमिक विद्यालय, महोत्तरी) was established in 1945 AD (2002 BS) as a community based educational institution, which is situated in Manara Shiswa Municipality-1, Manara, Mahottari, Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education. It offers educational programs from ECD to grade 10 and Plus Two (Class 11 and 12) in the Management, and Education streams.

Durga Secondary School, Mahottari provides up to secondary level education with moderate fee structures and also provides a scholarship scheme for the deserving students.

Why we choose?

Professional Teacher

Highly qualified, passionate, and innovative, our teachers at Durga Ma Vi provide personalized, engaging, and supportive education. Committed to continuous professional development and community involvement, they ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Project Based Learning

Our school embraces project-based learning, where students actively explore real-world problems and challenges. This dynamic approach fosters critical thinking, collaboration, and practical skills, preparing students for future success by turning theory into practice.

Science Labs

State-of-the-art, fully equipped, and designed for hands-on learning, our science labs at Durga Ma Vi foster curiosity and innovation. Providing a safe environment for experiments and discovery, they empower students to explore and excel in the sciences.
